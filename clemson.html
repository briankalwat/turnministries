The Hills Retreat

The Hills Retreat is an organization on a mission to get incoming Clemson freshman plugged into the body of Christ before they move into college.

Learn More

We provide a unique opportunity for students to meet fellow believers, learn more about campus ministries and churches available to them, and grow spiritually under the guidance of upperclassmen leaders at a 4-day, 3-night retreat.

The retreat takes place at Chimney Point in Westminster, SC which is approximately 40 minutes from Clemson's campus. During their stay, freshmen will engage in small group discussions, worship and speaker sessions, as well as large group games and activities. In addition, our Ministry Fair gives freshmen the chance to speak with local churches and ministries from the upstate. We are passionate about helping freshmen transition to college and aim to support and encourage them in a challenging season of life.

Each day will feature small group time, games and activities, speaker sessions, and worship.

Our passion is getting freshmen involved and connected with the body of Christ at Clemson. If finances are preventing your student from registering for the retreat, please email us at thehillsretreat@gmail.com so we can find a solution. We have scholarship opportunities available, and we feel strongly about meeting the needs of students who wish to come.
